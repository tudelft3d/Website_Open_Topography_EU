      Welcome to the European Topography Elevation Searcher

      Curated by Daan van der Heide, this platform serves as a visual exploration of topographic and point cloud datasets across Europe.

      As part of my PhD research on the harmonization of 3D geospatial data in Europe, this site provides an overview of available national datasets and their acquisition characteristics.

      Please note: this website is for browsing and reference purposes only. No datasets are hosted or available for direct download.
