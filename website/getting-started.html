Find your data on the map Find your data in the catalogue

1. Move and zoom the map

Drag to pan, scroll or pinch to zoom. Hold Shift + drag to tilt and rotate.

2. Select a country

Click any country on the map. The info panel shows available datasets and links.

3. View dataset details

See dataset type, accuracy, year, and the dataroom link for downloads.

4. Reset to overview

Use the HOME button in the top bar to return to the European overview.
